While we were at the Beach Club condo in Florida, there was an Osprey living at the pond outside our balcony.  He often perched on a tree directly across from our balcony, and I kept watch on him during the day while working.  One day I heard him screaming and saw him flying with a fish in his claws!  He was screaming his head off doing circles around the pond showing off his catch, so I snapped some photos.
